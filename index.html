GGreplayz

Automatically turn your SC2 game replays into web videos from multiple player perspectives.
No registration required.

Get started »

Step 1: Upload replay

Not familiar with these files? Your games are automatically saved as a file with a .SC2Replay extension. The files are small (typically < 100K).

Step 2: Set options

Specify how you want your replay video recorded. Perspectives, overlays and more.

Step 3: Relax

We do all the heavy lifting, and notify you when it is done. Get ready to show the world the cheese that failed - or watch the game you played last night, while on the crapper.
