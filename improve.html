Help make GGReplayz better

This site is new, and has no budget to run on. I do it in my spare time, and it all runs on terrible hardware. I'm working on a way to give video encoding priority to those who help out. Also, if this site ever takes off, I'll give back the hardware and find someway to thank you with more than just words.

If you are able to help, here is how:

Subscribe to my YouTube channel
The 15 minute video length limit adds complexity. subscribing to my channel will help get the length limits removed.
Got an old Nvidia video card (> 256MB RAM) you could donate?
Send me a message (private message is prefered) on Twitter: @rynop
Got an old box you can donate? I need Intel or AMD multi-core/multi-cpu boxes. More than 2GB RAM.
Send me a message (private message is prefered) on Twitter: @rynop
You can also by me a beer :)
Dwolla me @ @rynop
